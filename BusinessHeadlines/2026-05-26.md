### 4.1. Clones vs visitors: the “silent downloader” phenomenon

- **Visitors** are people who open the repo page.
- **Clones** are people (or systems) who **download the entire repository**.

When clones are high but visitors are low, it often means:

- People discover the repo via **direct links**, **scripts**, or **internal tools**, not by browsing GitHub search.
- Some users may be **mirroring** or **archiving** the repo for later study.
- Institutions or advanced users may be **batch‑downloading** multiple repos.

> “Now, these repositories have some 50–200 clones / full downloads from 25–100 computers respectively almost constantly… for already months.”
